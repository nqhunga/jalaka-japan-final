Install tips

Case? No case? You decide!

Jalaka is universal! It is suitable for almost all types of phones and cases. Leather, plastic, aluminum - no problem. It sticks on easily and securely with 2-sided adhesive tape provided with every product.

Surface can also be quite bumpy and the adhesive will hold! Check this phone straight out from one of our customers pocket. The use is rough and tough and we're still in the game.

How to install:

Just clean the rear surface of your device and attach Jalaka on it with pre-installed two sided adhesive tape. Could't be easier. With every Jalaka product you will also get two extra tapes so you can continue using our product, even if you change your mobile device.

Surfaces that we do not recommend for attaching Jalaka on:

Rubber/siliconecovers are nope. Its like butter, no sticker won't stay on it. These covers can be tested by stretching them. If it stretch easily, then it is not good for Jalaka. If it doesn't, then it's ok. TPU and TPE covers are ok for Jalaka. Learn more about the plastic differences.

Nano coating is pop, but for us it's not. Certain glass surfaces has an oleophobic surface treatment that decreases fingerprints but is not good for installing Jalaka. Read more about oleophobic surfaces

Super bumby surfaces. Imagine car tyres, the more surface on the road the more grip. No surface - no grip.
